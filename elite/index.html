The Elite Platoon

Qualify ☆ Compete ☆ Win

TTOC is known for its competitive atmosphere. This season, we've included a new bracket where only the best of the best can compete in. This competition consists of a different set of rules; be sure you understand them!

Elite Platoon Championship

  • Classic gamemode with all the weapons and bullets.
  • A round begins when Referee tanks are destroyed.
  • Double Elimination style bracket.
  • Total of 10 rounds, fight with all you've got!
  • Top 50% players will receive an exclusive accessory, not even crew members will have it!
  • The winner earns the title of Champion, and is featured at the bottom of this page.
  • Keep an eye out for the Elite Championship thread.

Are You Worthy?

Just a few things...

Bracket Information

Each player has the opportunity to lose one match, and still be able to win. However, losing twice would render the player eliminated. In the event of unmatchable times, be prepared to be flexible. By accepting the Wild Card, you acknowledge these rules.

How to Qualify

This is an 8 player bracket. Within the season, you must place in the Top 2 of any official gamemode. Placing in the Top 2 earns you a Wild Card. The player may decline their Wild Card, but this does not pass the Wild Card slot down to the next available player. The Wild Card will be unused.

Wild Card

It is possible for a player to earn multiple Wild Cards throughout the season. If a pre-qualified player earns a Wild Card, the Wild Card will be passed on to the next highest ranked player, who is not already qualifed. If this player chooses to decline the Wild Card, the Wild Card will be unused.

Match Info

This is a Double Elimination bracket. All players can only lose twice. Rankings will be determined by the results of the bracket. Please be keen on the schedule. A time and server will be posted below, unless otherwise noted. If a match does not have a qualifying time, be cooperative and work out a time.
