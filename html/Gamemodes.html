Official Gamemodes

TTOC is all about variety. That's why we have four gamemodes for players to compete in, to test players' skills. Each gamemode targets a different group of skills, while the Classic gamemode is an overall test. The gamemodes are played one after the other in TTOC, and each have their own separate sign up period. Depending on popularity, the gamemodes will be played in either 16 or 32 player single-eliminations.

Classic

The all-round favourite, classic gamemode. Putting overall skill to the test, this gamemode is the easiest to follow: survive by any means necessary in the TankTrouble Mazes!

  • Game Settings: Last Tank Standing, 4 players.
  • A normal battle with all the weapons and bullets.
  • A round begins when Referee tanks are destroyed.
  • Victor: First to 15 wins with a two win difference.

All bullets and weapons allowed

Tanks starting the round after Referees are destroyed

Weapons

Bullets are underkill. Destroy your opponent with the weapon of your choice. After all, that crate did spawn just for you! Go on... you know you want that weapon...

  • Game Settings: Last Tank Standing, 4 players.
  • A battle without bullets, only weapons from crates.
  • Both players start with only one weapon loaded. Players may only choose from the first 2 weapon crates that spawned.
  • Players will start in one of the outer corners of the maze, which they must "claim" by announcing it in chat, while being in the corner with a weapon.
  • Players may pick up and use all weapons infinitely after the round starts.
  • Referee tanks may only pick up crates to help clear paths or allow new weapon crates to spawn.
  • A round begins after:
    • Players are equipped with a weapon and
    • Player tanks are touching the outer maze wall with their turret in their claimed corner tile and
    • Referee tanks are destroyed
  • Victor: First to 15 wins with a 2 win difference.

All weapons allowed, no regular bullets

Shields allowed only after match begins

Tanks starting in the farthest corners of the maze

Golf

Five bullets is overkill. Slow down! Aim from where you are. The target: your opponent, of course. If you miss... well then, now you're the target. You can dodge by turning, but good luck with that!

  • Game Settings: Last Tank Standing, 4 players, Symmetrical.
  • Players shoot at each other at the same time, by only using turning controls.
  • Aiming, dodging and shooting is only allowed by pivoting on the spot. You may only move forward and back when travelling to your new shooting position.
  • Each shooting period will be started 15 seconds after players have settled into their shooting position, with the Referee counting down to zero. The last 3 seconds of the countdown will be in chat.
  • If both bullets despawn, each player will move to the location of their despawned bullet.
  • If bullets despawn within a three-block radius of eachother, the round is a draw.
  • Dispose of weapons if necessary.
  • If a player moves forward or back when not allowed, the opponent receives a point, and the round will not count.
  • Victor: First to 10 wins with a 2 win difference.

Only one bullet allowed at a time

Dispose of weapons to clear paths if necessary

Shooter aiming for their target

Gold Rush

It's the 1840s. You're caught amidst the Siberian Gold Rush! You must fight for all the gold, or receive a face full of lead. Rush for the gold or lose, which one will you choose?

  • Game Settings: Deathmatch, 4 players.
  • Players must earn the highest score by earning as many Dimitrium, Gold Coins, and Kills as possible.
  • Dimitrium is worth 6 points, Gold Coins are worth 3 points each, Kills are worth 1 point, but Suicides are -1 point.
  • Prevent your opponent from earning points with all bullets and weapons.
  • A round begins after the countdown. Referee tanks must turn in place wherever they spawn. Be aware of inactivity- You must turn both ways periodically to not be kicked.
  • Referee tanks must NOT interrupt gameplay by shooting bullets and/or otherwise.
  • The Referee will track and announce the round numbers at the beginning of the rounds.
  • Victor: Player with highest score after 15 rounds.

All bullets and weapons allowed

You can destroy your tank to respawn closer to gold...
